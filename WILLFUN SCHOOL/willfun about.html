Our History:

The WillFun School was founded in 2022 . It began as a nursery stage with an application of montessori. The vision was birthed by Dr, Williams Olufunke.

The school started from a room apartment with 3 pupils. The school grow slowly but steadily over the next few months and we have grown in size, stature and reputation.
